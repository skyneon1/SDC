Events

Engage, Innovate, Compete:
Join Our Exciting SDC Events!

At the Software Development Club (SDC), we are passionate about creating opportunities for growth and engagement through a variety of dynamic events. Our flagship Hackathons challenge participants to solve real-world problems and innovate under time constraints, fostering creativity and teamwork. We also host hands-on workshops that delve into key areas such as web development, Android development, and design, providing practical skills and in-depth knowledge to help you advance in these fields. In addition to our technical workshops, we organize eSports events where members can compete in popular games, enjoy friendly competition, and build camaraderie. These events are designed to cater to diverse interests and skill levels, ensuring there's something for everyone in the SDC community. Join us to learn, compete, and connect!

Achievements

Celebrating Excellence: SDC's Triumphs and Awards!

The Software Development Club (SDC) takes pride in our track record of success and recognition in the tech community. We have successfully conducted multiple hackathons, showcasing exceptional problem-solving skills and innovation among our members. Our team has also triumphed in hackathons hosted by other colleges, earning top honors and recognition for our creative solutions and technical prowess. These achievements reflect the hard work and dedication of our members and the vibrant spirit of SDC.
